Basics of Health Intelligent Data Analysis

2 Methods

Database used in this suty includes simulated data from 523 patients who were referred to sleep consultation for risk of obstructive sleep apnea. As two patients have no data for OSA, we considered on our analysis 521 patients. A total of 38 variables (table 2.1) were recorded as potential predictors for OSA.

Patient characteristics were described using contingency tables. Univariate tests comparing categorical variables were conducted using univariate logistic regression.
